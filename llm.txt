## Key UK Tax Rates (2025/26)
- Personal Allowance: £12,570
- Basic Rate: 20% (£12,571-£50,270)
- Higher Rate: 40% (£50,271-£125,140)
- Additional Rate: 45% (over £125,140)
- NI Class 1: 8% (£12,571-£50,270), 2% above
- Inheritance Tax: 40% above £325,000 nil-rate band
- RNRB: £175,000 (home to children)
- Stamp Duty: 0% up to £250k, 5% £250k-£925k, 10% £925k-£1.5M, 12% over £1.5M
