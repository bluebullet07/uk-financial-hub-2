How Our Calculators Work

Client-Side Processing

All calculations happen in your web browser using JavaScript. When you enter:

  • Your salary into the tax calculator
  • Your property value into the mortgage calculator
  • Your savings into the compound interest calculator

...these numbers are processed locally on your device. They are never sent to our servers.

Technical Explanation

How it works:

  1. You enter data into the calculator form
  2. JavaScript in your browser performs the calculations
  3. Results are displayed on your screen
  4. If you close the browser, all data is cleared (unless you save a PDF locally)

At no point does your data leave your device or get stored on our servers.
